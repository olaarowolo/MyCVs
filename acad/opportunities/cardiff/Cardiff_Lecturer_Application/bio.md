# Bio: Olasunkanmi Arowolo

## Olasunkanmi Arowolo

Olasunkanmi Arowolo is a recent PhD graduate from the **University of Kent's Centre for Journalism**, where he successfully defended his thesis with no corrections. He specializes in **media sociology** and explores research in:

- Digital journalism
- Citizen journalism
- Environmental communication
- Poverty discourse and social investment programs in Nigeria

### Academic and Professional Experience

With over **eight years** of experience as a media and communication scholar and journalist, Dr. Arowolo has held positions including:

- **Assistant Lecturer** at Lagos State University
- **Lecturer** at the University of Kent

### Publications and Research Impact

Dr. Arowolo has published extensively in leading journals including:

- **Global Knowledge, Memory and Communication** (Emerald Publishing)
- **Cogent Social Sciences** (Taylor & Francis)
- **East African Journal of Interdisciplinary Studies**

His research impact is reflected in:

- **260+ citations**
- **h-index of 5**
- **i10-index of 2**

### Professional Memberships

- Public Communication of Science and Technology (PCST)
- International Communication Association (ICA)
- Association of Communication Scholars and Professionals of Nigeria (ACSPN)
- Association of Media and Communication Researchers of Nigeria (AMCRON)

### Research Focus

Dr. Arowolo's research centers on **media representation of social issues**, with his professional experience as both journalist and academic directly informing his work in journalism and communication studies. His scholarly contributions address contemporary challenges in media and communication, particularly since 2020.
